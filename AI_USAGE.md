# AI usage disclosure

In the interest of transparency, this project discloses that **AI assistance was
used during its development**.

## What AI was used for

- **Debugging** — diagnosing and fixing issues (for example, tracing why the
  "Back up now" action failed and identifying that errors were being swallowed
  on the client, and resolving cross-platform/path issues during development).
- **Writing certain code passages** — drafting and refactoring selected parts of
  the codebase, including the SVG national-flag renderer, the coupon-collector
  pack-estimate maths, the backup/restore service and Settings UI, and assorted
  tests and documentation.

## Human oversight

All AI-assisted changes were **reviewed, tested and accepted by the project
maintainer** before being committed. The maintainer directed the work, made the
design decisions, and is responsible for the final code. AI was a tool in the
process — not an author of the project.
